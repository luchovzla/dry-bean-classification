Why Beans?


Through-out the world, beans are a mainstay for many diets. They are easy to grow, store, and prepare, in addition to being inexpensive. Beans come in many varieties. They’re packed with protein and fiber while low in fat and calories. In addition, they’re exceptionally high in a wide variety of vitamins and minerals.[1]
